# Score Cap Rules

Apply caps after provisional scoring. If multiple caps apply, use the lowest applicable cap unless Score Chair gives a documented evidence-based exception.

## Evidence Gap Caps
| Trigger | Technical Cap | Business Cap |
|---|---:|---:|
| Core component evidence missing | 22 | 24 |
| Effect verification absent | 24 | 24 |
| Mostly inventor assertions | 20 | 20 |
| Critical extraction confidence low/failed | 21 | 21 |
| Score claim lacks evidence ID | rescore item at 0–50% | rescore item at 0–50% |

## Competitor Comparison Caps
| Trigger | Technical Cap | Business Cap |
|---|---:|---:|
| No competitor comparison | 26 | 25 |
| Outdated or incomplete comparison | 27 | 26 |
| Invalid comparison target | 25 | 24 |

## Business Plan Caps
| Trigger | Business Cap |
|---|---:|
| No business plan | 22 |
| Business direction only, no execution plan | 24 |
| No customer/market evidence | 23 |
| No owner or schedule | 25 |

## Samsung SDS Product/Roadmap Caps
| Trigger | Business Cap |
|---|---:|
| No SDS product/roadmap evidence | 23 |
| Product relation exists but roadmap item unclear | 25 |
| Roadmap timing unclear | 26 |
| Only oral/internal assertion without document | 24 |

## Paper / Technology Trend Caps
| Trigger | Technical Cap | Business Cap |
|---|---:|---:|
| No paper and no external trend material | 27 | 28 |
| Trend source weakly related | 28 | 28 |
| Paper not connected to invention difference | 27 | no cap |

## Devil's Advocate Caps
| Trigger | Technical Cap | Business Cap |
|---|---:|---:|
| High-severity issue unresolved | 24 | 24 |
| Medium-severity issue unresolved | 27 | 27 |
| Maximum candidate without Devil's Advocate | 26 | 26 |
| Challenge is merely formal | 27 | 27 |

## Evidence Auditor Caps
Unsupported evidence is excluded. If an unsupported evidence item is central to an item score, rescore that item conservatively. If it is central to a maximum-score claim, maximum score is forbidden.
