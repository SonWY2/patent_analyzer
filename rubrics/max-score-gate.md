# Max Score Gate

## Objective Evidence Types Recognized for Maximum Scores
- Business plan.
- Samsung SDS product/technology/roadmap material.
- Paper.
- Competitor comparison and review result.
- External evaluation institution technology-trend material.

## Common Maximum-Score Requirements
- Evidence Auditor marks core evidence as `supported`.
- Devil's Advocate has raised substantive objections and high-severity objections are resolved.
- The score is not based on creative hypotheses alone.
- Extraction confidence for score-critical material is not low or failed.
- Missing information does not block the relevant conclusion.

## Technical Maximum Gate
Requires strong evidence for novelty/differentiation, implementation specificity, effect verifiability, and claim defensibility. At least one of paper, external technology trend, prior-art/attorney review, or competitor comparison must directly support the technical distinction.

## Business Maximum Gate
Requires business plan evidence and Samsung SDS product/roadmap evidence. Competitor comparison and market/customer evidence must support the claimed utilization advantage.

## Blocking Conditions
- No Devil's Advocate review.
- Unresolved high-severity Devil's Advocate issue.
- Unsupported core evidence.
- Business max requested without business plan.
- Business max requested without SDS product/roadmap evidence.
- Technical max requested without prior-art, paper, trend, or competitor comparison support.
